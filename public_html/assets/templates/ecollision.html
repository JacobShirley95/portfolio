ECollision

View demo GitHub

ECollision is a basic elastic/inelastic collision simulation library that I made for my final year A Level project. It is designed to help students understand the laws of energy conservation and momentum by controlling the velocity and radius of particles in the simulation.

ECollision has gone through many iterations over the years since I left sixth-form. It was firstly written in ES5 JavaScript, but I then decided to move over to CoffeeScript, as this has better support for a class syntax. It is currently being ported to the ES6 version of JavaScript, which is now on par with CoffeeScript in features. of JavaScript from CoffeeScript.

The background of this portfolio site uses ECollision to simulation the particles moving around the screen.
